Two linked strands of biological modelling: reconstructing biochemical metabolic
pathways, and studying how cells self-organise and sort in the
[Cellular Potts](https://journals.aps.org/prl/abstract/10.1103/PhysRevLett.69.2013)
model.

Starting from enzyme kinetics and building up to the dynamics of small

## Pattern formation

A Schnakenberg reaction–diffusion system: two chemicals diffusing and reacting
at different rates spontaneously break symmetry into stable spatial patterns —
a Turing mechanism for biological patterning.

  gif
  src="/media/group-metabolic-cell-sorting/schnakenberg.mp4"

## Cell sorting — the Cellular Potts model

How differential adhesion alone drives two intermixed cell types to separate.
