A study of how opinions form and spread in social structures, following
[social impact theory](https://psycnet.apa.org/record/1982-01296-001). We analysed
critical temperature, self-organised criticality, and the conditions under which
opinions unify.

Code and details [on GitHub](https://github.com/jonas4climate/complex-systems-project).
