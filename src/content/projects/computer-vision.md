For a Royal Society Industry Summer Studentship at the University of Birmingham,
I designed a monitoring system to detect **abnormal animal behaviour in
captivity** from CCTV, using pose estimation.

Supervised by
[Dr. Jackie Chappell](https://www.jackiechappell.com/), Senior Lecturer in Animal
Behaviour and leader of the Cognitive Adaptations Research Group, the tool helps
keepers flag unusual behaviour automatically and can be adapted to their specific
behaviour-data needs.
