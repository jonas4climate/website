In June 2022 I represented ClimateScience at [UN SB56](https://unfccc.int/SB56), the Bonn Climate
Conference — the mid-year session of the UNFCCC subsidiary bodies that prepares
the ground for the annual COP. The main work involved networking with potential partners.
