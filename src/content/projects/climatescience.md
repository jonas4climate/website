Across several roles at ClimateScience — a volunteer-driven climate-education
nonprofit — I worked at the intersection of people, partnerships and product.

## Head of HR (2022 — 2023)

On the executive coordination team, leading HR operationally and strategically.
I built an HR strategy grounded in kindness to maximise engagement and well-being
across **500+ remote volunteers in 50 countries** — introducing internal
newsletters, recruiting pipelines and outreach teams, volunteer training
programmes, and feedback and assessment cycles. The role also covered
facilitating corporate consultancy workshops and work across finance, fundraising
and operational automation.

## Partnerships (2020 — 2022)

As European and German Partnerships Lead, I established governmental
collaborations in Germany and Austria — including the organisation's first
governmental partnership in Europe — and led a team of ten to build **35+
national volunteer communities** worldwide, while securing philanthropic funding.

## Web development (2019 — 2020)

Earlier, I helped build the website's front-end and login utilities in TypeScript
as part of a four-person team, and supported backend administration.
