In a team of seven, ClimateScience hosted its first-ever virtual **Community
Conference** — tens of events ranging from casual short sessions to expert
speeches, with several hundred registrations.

I arranged the attendance and on-the-day experience of expert speakers, spoke at
some of the events myself, and helped with moderation.
